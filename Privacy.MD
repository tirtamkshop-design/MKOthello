# MK Othello – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29th Nov 2025

Welcome to MK Othello for Android, developed by mkshop.

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the MK Othello app does and does not collect.

## 1. Data Collected by the App

MK Othello does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- The app does not request or access any personal information.
- All game data and settings are stored locally on your device.
- Uninstalling the app or clearing the app data will remove all locally stored settings.
- The app does not require an internet connection to function, except when downloading or updating the app through the Google Play Store.

## 2. Permissions

This app does not request or require any special permissions to function.

## 3. Google Play Store Analytics

The MK Othello app itself does not collect any analytics, logs, or device information.

However, Google Play Store and the Android operating system may independently collect:
- installation statistics
- crash reports
- basic diagnostic information

These are handled by Google and are not controlled by the MK Othello app or mkshop. No third-party analytics libraries are used in the app.

For more information on Google’s data collection, please refer to the Google Play Privacy and Security documentation.

## 4. Children’s Privacy

MK Othello does not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children under the age of 13.

The app does not include:
- user accounts
- online features
- chat or messaging
- data tracking
- third-party analytics
- advertisements

Because no personal data is collected, the app is safe for users of all ages.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on the GitHub page where this policy is hosted.
